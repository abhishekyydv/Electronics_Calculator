How This Calculator Works

This calculator determines the gain and output voltage of an operational amplifier in two configurations:

Non-Inverting:
Gain: Gain = 1 + (Rf / Rin)
Output: Vout = Gain × Vin

Inverting:
Gain: Gain = –(Rf / Rin)
Output: Vout = Gain × Vin
