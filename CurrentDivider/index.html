How This Calculator Works

In a parallel resistor network with total current \( I \), the current through resistor \( R_n \) is calculated as: 

I_n = (R_total / R_n) * (I / sum(R_total))

Where:
• \( I \) is the total input current
• \( R_n \) is the resistance of the nth resistor
• Total current is divided based on the resistance values (inversely proportional).
