How This Calculator Works

  • cd = mcd ÷ 1000
  • Luminous Flux (lm):
    lm = 4π · cd · (1 − cos(beamAngle ÷ 2))
  • Lux (if distance provided):
    lux = lm ÷ (4π · distance²)

Formulas assume a conical beam pattern and isotropic emission within that cone.
